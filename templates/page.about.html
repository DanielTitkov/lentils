Orrery

Psychometrics for fun and science

Orrery is private psychometric lab, focused on developing useful inventories with high psychometric qualities.

We strive to fulfil the needs of both researchers and end-users

For end-users we provide shareable detailed reports and friendly interpretations

For researchers we aim to provide instruments of conduction researches, flexible standardizations and analysis tools (currently under development).
